🔒 Privacy Policy

How ZakatCalculation.in handles your information

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

ZakatCalculation.in ("we", "our", or "the website") is a free, non-commercial web tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you visit our website, and how it is used.

By using ZakatCalculation.in, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal financial data entered into the Zakat calculator (all calculations run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers)
  • Names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Account information (there is no user account system)
  • Payment or banking information

3. Information We Collect via Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects the following types of data automatically:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Referring website (where you came from)

This data is collected anonymously and is used solely to improve the website. We do not use this data to identify you personally. Google Analytics data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to track session information. These are third-party cookies managed by Google. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not set any first-party cookies ourselves. Your calculator preferences (language, currency) are not stored between sessions.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics – for website usage statistics (see Section 3)
  • Google Fonts – for typography (Poppins and Noto Sans Devanagari fonts are loaded from Google's servers)

These services may collect data as per their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

6. Data Security

Since all Zakat calculations are performed entirely in your browser (client-side JavaScript), your financial data never leaves your device. We do not have access to any values you enter into the calculator.

7. Children's Privacy

ZakatCalculation.in does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The website is a general-use tool intended for adult Muslims.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

This calculator provides a general estimate. Consult a qualified scholar for complex cases.
