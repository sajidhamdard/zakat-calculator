About ZakatCalculation.in

A free, open-use Zakat calculator for Muslims worldwide

About This Tool

ZakatCalculation.in is a free online Zakat calculator designed to help Muslims easily calculate their annual Zakat al-Mal (wealth Zakat). The tool supports gold, silver, cash, bank balances, investments, business stock, and property assets - with deductions for liabilities.

The calculator supports both the silver Nisab and gold Nisab methods, making it suitable for followers of different scholarly opinions. It also supports 10 languages and 11 currencies, ensuring accessibility for Muslims around the world.

💡 Formula used: (Total Zakatable Assets − Payable Liabilities) × 2.5%
Zakat becomes obligatory when net wealth reaches or exceeds the Nisab threshold and is maintained for one full lunar year (Hawl).

Features

  • 🌐 Available in 10 languages: Hindi, English, Arabic, Spanish, French, Chinese, Bengali, Portuguese, Russian, Urdu
  • 💰 Supports 11 currencies: INR, USD, EUR, GBP, SAR, AED, PKR, BDT, IDR, CNY, MYR
  • 📊 Calculates Zakat on gold, silver, cash, bank balance, investments, and business stock
  • ⚖️ Supports both Silver Nisab and Gold Nisab methods
  • 📅 Includes option for previous year's pending Zakat
  • 📱 Fully mobile responsive with a clean, modern UI
  • 🆓 Completely free - no sign-up, no ads blocking content

About the Developer

This tool was developed by Sajid Ali Khan as a public service for the Muslim community. The goal is to make Zakat calculation simple, accurate, and accessible for everyone - whether you are in India, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, or anywhere else in the world.

This is a non-commercial project. The calculator provides a general estimate based on standard Islamic jurisprudence. For complex financial situations, please consult a qualified Islamic scholar.
